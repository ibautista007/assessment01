El Salvador is a country enriched with many stories either before or after the Spanish conquest.
Pre conquest, we can find ruins of the Mayan culture.
Post conquest, there are many places where several battles were carried out with the purpose of the independence of the country, in addition to civil war within it for the seizure of power.

Although El Salvador is a country with little territorial space, it is a country that has very impressive forest tourist places. El Pital is the coldest and highest mountain in the country, until the point of snow falling. Which is a tropical country is very surprising.
There are many places where you can go hiking and one of the most outstanding is the road to the volcanic Lake of Coatepeque. After making the walk you can enjoy the tourist beauty of the lake.
