About Us


Tour Torogoz El Salavdor is a non-profit company, in order to provide more information about the beautiful tourist places that the country of El Salvador has Our company was born when the founder Isaias Bautista lived outside of El Salvador. He realized the beauties of his native country, so he considered promoting these beautiful places to provide the opportunity for Salvadorans to know these beautiful places in the country and foreigners to provide a personalized and accessible guide to the most beautiful places in El Salvador.


Our Purpose & Values


Provide a personalized guide to tourists who visit El Salvador or for Salvadorans who wish to know more about of all the beauties of the country. We provide 3 main 3 main alternatives that offers 3 totally different experiences, for those who love the sea, those who love the forest or those who love history. Our main focus is to provide a pleasant experience on your visit ... to receive you back in the country.


Our Unique Features


Our staff has more than necessary knowledge about the places we serve. They have experienced this tour package previously in order to know what the main needs of tourists are. Our staff can communicate primarily in English and Spanish.
