About Us


Safi Foods is a registered business in British Columbia, Canada; we import quality food for Canadians under the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) license and required standards. We are a B2B distributor selling to a growing number of companies throughout Canada. We are dedicated to import quality and affordable foods from Turkey and sell to wholesalers, natural health product shops, pharmacies, and retailer shops. We concentrate on products imported from Turkey because these products are produced with European Union (EU) standards, which are highly acceptable in Canada at a lower price than Canadian products.


Our Purpose & Values


We envision importing quality foods at affordable prices to Canadians. We want to have a considerable role in the Canadian food supply chain. We deliver excellence by importing high-quality products that meet all Canadian Standards at a price below the cost of similar Canadian products. Our service philosophy is that we deliver excellence for our customers, and our business is based on professionalism, business ethics, and morality.


Our Unique Features


Our premium quality products are unique in the market and priced competitively; and are produced under European Union (EU) standards with BPA-free packaging, which is important to many people in Canada. Also, our products are Halal certified, which is of great importance to the Muslim population.
